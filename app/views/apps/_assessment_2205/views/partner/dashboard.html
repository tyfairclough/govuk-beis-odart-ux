What are activities?

The language used to describe things such as programmes, grants and calls varies across organisations. We use the following definitions:

Programmes

Programmes are the aims or subject areas that funds like Newton and GCRF raise funding against. Calls to tender are made under these headings for delivery partners to deliver agianst.

Projects

Projects are the initiatives that feed into the programme. They are operated by delivery partners who issue calls to tender activities against these projects.

Activites

Activities represent the end of the supply chain spending. Activities have a principal investigator who delivers against the funding project and programme goals.
